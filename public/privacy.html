Ulahia — Simple Shop Book

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

1. What we collect

Ulahia stores the business records you create: your shop details, products, sales, customers, debts, and expenses. Your data is stored on your device first, and optionally backed up to secure cloud storage so you can restore it with your recovery code.

2. What we never do

We never sell your data. We never share your business records with third parties. We never contact your customers.

3. How your data is protected

Cloud backups are encrypted in transit and protected by your recovery code. Only someone with your recovery code can restore your data.

4. Your control

You can export or delete your data at any time from Settings inside the app.

5. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us from the Help & Support section in the app.
